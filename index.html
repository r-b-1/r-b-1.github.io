Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How does the pricing work?

Our pricing is subscription-based with monthly or annual billing options. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time. We also offer a 14-day free trial so you can test our software before committing.

Is there a setup fee?

No, there are no setup fees. You only pay for your subscription. Our team will help you get started at no additional cost.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees. If you cancel, you'll have access until the end of your billing period.

Do you offer customer support?

Yes, we offer customer support via email, live chat, and phone. The level of support depends on your subscription plan. Enterprise customers receive 24/7 dedicated support.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect your data. Our systems are regularly audited and we're compliant with major security standards.

Can I integrate with other tools?

Yes, our software integrates with many popular tools and platforms. We offer API access on our Enterprise plan for custom integrations. Check our documentation for a full list of supported integrations.
